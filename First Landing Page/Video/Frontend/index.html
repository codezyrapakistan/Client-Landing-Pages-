About TVS Emerald Auralis

TVS Emerald Auralis is the latest premium residential development by TVS Emerald located in the rapidly growing neighborhood of Thanisandra, North Bangalore. Blending modern architecture with smart urban living, this project promises luxury, connectivity, and convenience in one of Bangalore's most sought-after locations. Prime Location of TVS Emerald Auralis is strategically located near Manyata Tech Park, ORR, and Kempegowda International Airport, making it ideal for IT professionals and frequent flyers.
